== 📋 Project Overview

This repository contained the component responsible for handling ITL integrations within the UK Emissions Trading Scheme (UK ETS) ecosystem. 

Specifically, this was the:
* *uk-ets-app-itl-receiving-endpoint* (Handles incoming requests from the ITL)

== 🏛️ Historical Context

The Kyoto International Transaction Log (ITL) was a central system managed by the UNFCCC to validate transactions under the Kyoto Protocol. With the evolution of the UK ETS and the ultimate decommissioning of the ITL infrastructure, the services bridging the UK registry to the ITL became obsolete. 

Thank you to everyone who contributed to the development and maintenance of this service during its active lifecycle.
