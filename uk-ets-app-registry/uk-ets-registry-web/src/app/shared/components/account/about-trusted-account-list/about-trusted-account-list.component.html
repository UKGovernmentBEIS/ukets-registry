About the trusted account list

Unless you change the default transaction rules:

  • transfers of units to trusted accounts must be approved by a second Authorised Representative
  • you will not be able to carry out transfers of units to accounts that are not on the trusted account list
  • surrender transactions or return of excess allocations must be approved by a second Authorised Representative
  • Accounts from the same account holder are automatically added to the trusted account list.
  • Once an account is opened, you can add accounts from different account holders to the trusted account list.
  • The addition or removal of a trusted account from your Trusted Account List must be approved by a second Authorised Representative with either Initiate and Approve or Approve permissions. Addition and removal requests that have not yet been approved will be displayed as requiring ‘Approval’ in the Pending column of the Trusted Account List.
