UK Emissions Trading Registry guidance

Document upload requests

If the Registry Administrator or National Administrator needs to see any documents in relation to your application, you will receive an email with the title 'Notification of documentation request - UK Emissions Trading Registry'.

Uploading documents

To upload your documents:

  • sign in to the UK Registry
  • select Tasks from the header bar
  • select the Request ID

You must upload the documents to the UK Registry using an existing PDF template, or as a PDF document if no template is available.

PDF templates

Templates for the following documents are available on the UK Registry:
  1. Letter of authority
  2. Primary Contact appointment and declaration
  3. Authorised Representative declaration

The Registry Administrator or National Administrator will send you a request by email if any of the following documents are needed:

  • List of directors
  • Organisation chart
  • Proof of company registration
  • Proof of name
  • Proof of permanent residential address
  • Criminal record evidence
  • Audited company accounts

When you have uploaded the requested documents, select Complete task at the top of the page.
