UK Emissions Trading Registry guidance

Request to open a voluntary account

The UK Registry only allows for applications to open voluntary UK ETS Trading Accounts, or UK KP Registry Person Holding Accounts. Compliance accounts will be opened by the Registry Administrator for participants in the UK ETS on instruction from the respective regulator. The process to apply to open a UK ETS Trading Account, or UK KP Person Holding Account is identical. The example given is for an application to open a UK ETS Trading Account.

Opening a voluntary account

Voluntary account holders (ETS Traders and KP users):

  1. Create a UK Registry sign in to obtain a User ID
  2. Apply to open an account (Trading or Person Holding Account)
  3. The Registry Administrator or National Administrator will request documents via the UK Registry - you will receive an email
  4. You upload documents as requested
  5. The Registry Administrator or National Administrator will review the documentation, and if fit and proper you will become ‘Validated’
  6. You will be sent a Registry Activation Code (RAC) by email
  7. Log in to the UK Registry and enter the RAC to become ‘Enrolled’

Opening a voluntary account - step by step guidance

Select Accounts at the top of the page

You must complete the sections in order.

The request must be completed in one session, you cannot save and return to this screen

Choose the account holder type

If the Account Holder is a corporate entity, such as an organisation, you’ll need to enter the:

  • legal entity name
  • registered address
  • company registration number

If the Account Holder is an individual, you’ll need to enter their:

  • full name
  • country of birth
  • date of birth
  • home address
  • phone number
  • email address

The Account Holder must be at least 18 years old.

Choosing the transaction rules

The default transaction rules are:

  • transfers of allowance or Kyoto units to trusted accounts must be approved by a second Authorised Representative
  • transfers of allowances or Kyoto units can only be made to accounts that are on the Trusted Account List (TAL)

The UK ETS Registry only holds allowances, and the UK KP Registry only holds Kyoto units.

When you request to open a Trading Account or Person Holding Account you can change these default transaction rules so that:

  • transfers do not have to be approved if they are being made to a trusted account
  • allowances or Kyoto units can be transferred to accounts that are not on the Trusted Account List
