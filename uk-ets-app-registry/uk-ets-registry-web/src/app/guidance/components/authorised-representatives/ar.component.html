UK Emissions Trading Registry guidance

Authorised Representatives

Authorised Representatives are individuals added to an account who can act on behalf of the account holder.

Contents

  • - Adding Authorised Representatives
  • - Adding an additional Authorised Representative to an existing account
  • - Authorised Representatives on Operator Holding Accounts and Aircraft Operator Holding Accounts

Adding Authorised Representatives

A UK ETS Trading Account or UK KP Person Holding Account must have at least 2 Authorised Representatives added to the account at the application stage:

  • one Authorised Representative with permission to initiate, or initiate and approve transactions
  • a different Authorised Representative with permission to approve, or initiate and approve transactions

You can add up to 8 Authorised Representatives on an account.

Adding an additional Authorised Representative to an existing account

  1. Create a UK Registry sign in to obtain a User ID
  2. Provide your User ID to an existing Authorised Representative with access to the account
  3. The Registry Administrator or National Administrator will request documents via the UK Registry - you will receive an email
  4. You upload documents as requested
  5. The Registry Administrator or National Administrator will review the documentation, and if fit and proper you will become ‘Validated’
  6. You will be sent a Registry Activation Code (RAC) by email
  7. Log in to the UK Registry and enter the RAC to become ‘Enrolled’

Authorised Representatives on Operator Holding Accounts and Aircraft Operator Holding Accounts

Once you are enrolled on the Registry, a Registry Administrator will contact you to assign you as an Authorised Representative on the relevant account for your organisation.

You will be asked to upload supporting documentation to the Registry to verify your identity and complete the approval process.

You’ll receive an email specifying the documents to be uploaded. The subject of the email is ‘Notification of documentation request - UK Emissions Trading Registry’.
