UK Emissions Trading Registry guidance

Registration status

After you create a UK Registry sign in you will have limited access to the UK Registry as a registered user. To gain access to your account in the UK ETS Registry or UK KP Registry you must go through validation to become an enrolled user.

Contents

  • - User ID
  • - Enrolled users

Your registration status

You can check your registration status in the My profile section

User ID

You can find your User ID in the My Profile section. This will also show your registration status.

Enrolled users

After you successfully enter your Registry Activation Code (RAC) you will be automatically signed out of the UK Registry. When you next sign in you will have full access to the UK Registry functions available for your role and your user status will change to Enrolled.
