About the UK Emissions Trading Registry

The UK Emissions Trading Registry (UK Registry) comprises:

  • the UK Emissions Trading Scheme Registry (UK ETS Registry)
  • the UK Kyoto Protocol Registry (UK KP Registry)

About the Registries
