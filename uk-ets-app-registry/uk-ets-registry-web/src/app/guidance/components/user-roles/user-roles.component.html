UK Emissions Trading Registry guidance

User roles

There are different user roles within the UK Registry. A registry user may have a different role on each account they are associated with.

Registry Administrator and National Administrator

The UK ETS Registry is administered by the Environment Agency performing the functions of the Registry Administrator. The UK KP Registry is administered by the Environment Agency in its capacity as National Administrator.

Account Holder

The Account Holder is the corporate entity (for example a company) or individual (for example a sole trader) that holds the account.

Primary Contact

All Account Holders will be asked to appoint a Primary Contact. A Primary Contact must have the authority to sign on behalf of the Account Holder, for example a director or someone named in the scheme of delegation of a corporate Account Holder. The Primary Contact will be the first person contacted by the Registry Administrator or the National Administrator about the account. The Primary Contact does not have access to the UK Registry by default, unless the Account Holder nominates them as an Authorised Representative.

Alternative Primary Contact

The Account Holder may also appoint an alternative Primary Contact. If the Primary Contact is unavailable, the Registry Administrator or National Administrator will contact the alternative Primary Contact. The alternative Primary Contact does not have access to the UK Registry by default, unless the Account Holder nominates them as an Authorised Representative.

Authorised Representative

An Authorised Representative can act on behalf of the account holder, if they have permission to do so. Authorised Representatives can be given permission to:

  • initiate transactions
  • approve transactions
  • both initiate and approve transactions
  • have read only access
