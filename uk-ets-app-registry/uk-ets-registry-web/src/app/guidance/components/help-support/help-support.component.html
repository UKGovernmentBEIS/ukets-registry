UK Emissions Trading Registry guidance

Registry help and support

Help and support is available for the UK Registry.

If you need further help accessing or using the UK Emissions Trading Registry, email the UK Registry Helpdesk. Include the subject 'UK Registry help request' in the email header, and your User ID and account details in the body of the email.

The UK Registry Helpdesk is available Monday to Friday, 8:30am to 4:30pm (except public holidays in England).
