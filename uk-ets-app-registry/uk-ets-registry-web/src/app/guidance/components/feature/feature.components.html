Managing your profile

You can update your email address, password and two-factor authentication app in the UK Registry by selecting ‘My Profile’ from the top menu and following the instructions shown on the page.

To change other information including your personal or work details, email the UK Registry Helpdesk.

Automatic timeout

If you’re inactive on the UK Registry for 30 minutes you will be automatically signed out for security reasons. A warning will appear before your session ends.
