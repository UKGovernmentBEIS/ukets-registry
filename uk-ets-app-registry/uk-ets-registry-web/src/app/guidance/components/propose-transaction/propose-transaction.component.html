Transfering allowances or Kyoto units

To transfer allowances or Kyoto units, you will need:

  • the account number of the account that will receive the allowances, for example UK-123-123456-1-12
  • access to the FreeOTP Authenticator app

Select Accounts at the top of the page and choose the account that you want to transfer allowances or Kyoto units from

Under the Overview for the account, select Propose transaction

Enter the number of allowances or Kyoto units you want to transfer

Enter the account number of the acquiring account

You will be able to check the transfer proposal before you submit it. To confirm a transaction proposal, you will need to generate and enter a two-factor authentication code using the FreeOTP Authenticator app. This is known as ‘digital signing’ and provides additional security for transactions within the UK Registry.

After you sign and submit the proposal, it will need to be approved by another Authorised Representative with the correct permissions on the account.
