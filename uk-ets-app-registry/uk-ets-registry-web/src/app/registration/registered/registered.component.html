When will I need my user ID?

You will need your user ID if you:

  • forget your password
  • want to change your password
  • are nominated as an authorised representative for an account
When you sign in to the Registry for the first time, you'll be asked to set up two-factor authentication using the FreeOTP Authenticator app.
You'll be able to view guidance on how to download and use the app.

What happens next

You currently have limited access to the UK Emissions Trading Registry. You can:

  • request to open a registry account
  • change your password
  • change your personal details
  • change your work details

An enrolled registry user can nominate you as an authorised representative for a registry account if you have given them your user ID.

You'll receive a Registry activation code by email if your registration is approved by a registry administrator. The approval process can take up to 10 days.

You must use your Registry activation code to activate your Registry Sign-in within 2 months from the date of the email. If you lose your code, or it expires before you have activated your Registry Sign-in, you must sign in to the registry and request a new code.
