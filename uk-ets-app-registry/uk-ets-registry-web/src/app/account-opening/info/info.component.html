Request to open an Emissions Trading Registry account

Request to open a new account under the Emission Trading Scheme (ETS) or the Kyoto Protocol (KP) scheme.

It will take approximately 15 minutes to complete the request.

Before you start

If you require an Operator Holding Account, Aircraft Operator Holding Account or Maritime Operator Holding Account to meet your surrender obligation please contact the UK ETS Registry Helpdesk ({{ helpdeskEmail$ | async | lowercase }}) as your account will have been opened on your behalf by the Registry Administrator.

In any other case, you'll need to give information about:
  • the account holder, Primary Contact and representatives
  • the business or operator to be registered
